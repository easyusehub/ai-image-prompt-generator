## 🚀 How It Works

1. **Enter Your Vision:** Type a simple description of the image you want.
2. **Customize Styles:** Select your preferred art style, lighting, and camera settings.
3. **Engage AI Engine:** Click the generate button to build your professional prompt.
4. **Copy & Create:** Use the generated prompt in your favorite AI Art generator.
